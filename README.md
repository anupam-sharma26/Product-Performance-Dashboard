# Product Performance Dashboard

## Overview
This dashboard analyzes Sephora product performance across brands, SKUs, sales channels, and countries. It provides a comprehensive view of revenue distribution, top-performing products, and brand performance to help business teams understand product trends and sales drivers.

## Business Objective
The goal of this dashboard is to help teams:
- Identify top-performing products and brands
- Monitor overall product revenue performance
- Analyze sales distribution across countries
- Compare performance between web and app channels
- Track revenue trends over time

## Tools Used
Power BI  
Power Query  
DAX  
SQL / Data Transformation  

## Key Metrics
Gross Revenue (AED) – Total revenue generated from product sales  
Total Unique SKUs – Number of unique products sold  
Total Brands – Number of brands contributing to revenue  
Revenue by Brand – Sales contribution by each brand  
Revenue by SKU – Performance of individual products  

## Dashboard Insights
- Huda Beauty generates the highest revenue among brands.
- A small number of SKUs contribute significantly to total revenue.
- Revenue is almost evenly distributed between web and app channels.
- Saudi Arabia and UAE contribute the majority of total revenue.
- Monthly revenue trends show steady growth in sales.

## Business Value
This dashboard helps teams:
- Monitor product performance across brands
- Identify high-performing SKUs
- Understand regional sales distribution
- Optimize product strategy and marketing focus
- Support data-driven business decisions
